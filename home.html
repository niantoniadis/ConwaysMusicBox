Welcome To Conway's Music Box!

Instructions

  • Click on the "App" tab to start making tunes!
  • You can save your creations by clicking "Save Original" (to save it from the start), or by clicking "Save Current" (to save the current grid).
  • Click on individual squares to finetune your melody.
  • View other people's creations and share your own on the community tab!
